Key Features

Powerful Token Gates

Powerful features that make managing exclusive communities effortless and secure

🔐
Token-Based Access
Gate your community on any ERC-20, SPL, or Jetton token. Members must hold the minimum to enter and stay.
💳
USD-Denominated Gates
Set thresholds in USD — the bot prices tokens live via DexScreener. Gate on $50 of value, not raw amounts.
🛡️
Secure Wallet Verification
Ownership proven by a tiny self-transfer — no seed phrases, no browser extensions, no custody of funds.
👤
Admin Whitelisting
Provide permanent access for admins and trusted users without the need for token verification.
🧹
Automatic Purging
Monthly sweeps automatically remove users who no longer meet your gate requirements. No manual work.
⛓️
Multichain Token Support
ETH, Base, Solana, TON — one bot handles all chains. Projects with bridged tokens are treated as a single gate.

How It Works

Simple 4-step process to secure your Telegram communities with blockchain-powered access control

1
Create Your Gate
Define the token, network, and USD threshold requirements for your gate. Takes under 2 minutes.
2
Users Verify or Pay
Users verify their wallet via a self-transfer dust proof — no extensions, no seed phrases, no custody.
3
Access is Granted
The bot checks on-chain balance against your threshold and approves the join request instantly.
4
Daily Purges Maintain Standards
The bot automatically removes users who no longer meet the requirements — no loopholes.
