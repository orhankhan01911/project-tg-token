what is gatekeeper

Permissioned communities.
Verified by your wallet.

GATEKEEPER is a non-custodial token-gating service for Telegram. Community owners set a token threshold on any EVM, Solana, or TON asset and the bot enforces it on every join request, automatically.

Verification runs via a self-transfer dust proof: you send a tiny amount to your own address, proving wallet ownership on-chain. No browser extensions, no seed phrases, no custody of funds.

Membership is continuously enforced. Sell your tokens or leave the group and access is revoked on the next monthly sweep. No manual moderation. No loopholes.

how it works

Four steps. No seed phrases.
No browser extensions.

01 // CLICK
Use the invite link
The group owner shares a Telegram invite link. You click it to request entry.
02 // VERIFY
Prove your wallet
Bot DMs you with a dust-transfer instruction. Send a micro-tx to yourself on any supported chain.
03 // GATE
Balance check
We verify your on-chain balance against the group's token threshold in real time.
04 // ACCESS
In or out
Pass → approved instantly. Fail → clear decline message with exactly what's needed.
