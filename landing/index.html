Process

Four steps. No seed phrases.
No browser extensions.

Ownership proven by a tiny self-transfer — your keys never leave your wallet.

01
Click
Use the invite link
The group owner shares a Telegram invite link. Click it to request entry to the gated group.
02
Verify
Prove your wallet
Send a tiny amount from your wallet to itself. No extensions, no signatures, no seed phrases required.
03
Gate
Balance check
The bot reads your on-chain holdings and checks them against the group's USD-denominated threshold.
04
Access
In or out
Pass → approved instantly. Fail → clear decline message showing exactly which token you need.
Features

Powerful gating.
Zero compromise.

Everything you need to run an exclusive, always-enforced token community.

🔐
Non-custodial Auth
We never touch your keys, funds, or wallet connections. Ownership proven by self-transfer — purely on-chain.
⛓️
Multi-chain Support
ETH, Base, Solana, and TON. One bot, all networks. Gate on ERC-20, SPL tokens, or TON Jettons.
💰
USD-Denominated Gates
Set gates in USD — the bot prices tokens live via DexScreener. Gate on $50 of any token, not a raw amount.
🧹
Automatic Purging
Monthly sweeps remove members who sold their tokens. No manual checks, no loopholes, no appeals.
⚡
2-Minute Setup
Add the bot as admin, run /setup, define your token gate. Done. No dashboard, no code, no integration work.
🛡️
Re-verify on Rejoin
Members who leave and try to rejoin are verified from scratch. No stale access, no forwarded invite exploits.
